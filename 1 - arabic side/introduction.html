هدف +

The purpose of this manual is to provide people at Alturki with clear and effective policies. These policies must reflect the culture of the organization. They are intended to guide our daily behavior and facilitate operations and decision making. Any terms and conditions of employment not covered, in part or in whole, in this manual shall be subject to the provisions of the prevailing Labor Law of Saudi Arabia. Moreover, the policies in this manual shall be subject to change from time to time at the discretion of Alturki’s management and shall be reviewed on an annual basis.

رؤيتنا +

To be recognized as the premier investor and partner for building great sustainable businesses in Saudi Arabia and Egypt.

مهمتنا +

To accelerate the sustainable growth of our businesses.

We will achieve our Mission by:

  • Wisely allocating capital, engaging talented people, and capturing and sharing knowledge; and
  • Striving to make each day better and joyful for all stakeholders.
الثقافة لدينا +

PERFORM to create value every day; and promise each year to be better than the year before!

We believe in delivering high performance in accordance with these seven commitments:

Passion

We take a deep personal interest to act purposefully to achieve excellence and success.


Measurable Indicators:

  • Equipped with confidence for doing what we love for a living;
  • Highly spirited, driven and always up for our next adventure;
  • Strive for the highest personal achievement in all aspects;
  • Act with urgency by showing a can-do attitude when met with challenges so we can exceed expectations.

Respect

We respect all our stakeholders and we respect our environment.


Measurable Indicators:

  • Consistently treat people with respect, fairness and equality;
  • Nurture our people’s strengths and provide opportunities for development and personal growth;
  • Create an inclusive environment that respects diversity and experience;
  • Contribute to the improvement of the environments in which we operate.

Fun

We bring fun into our corporate environment, recognize efforts and celebrate achievements.


Measurable Indicators:

  • Build an environment that promotes positive energy and enthusiasm;
  • Encourage teamwork;
  • Remembers that “Wow” is a desirable outcome;
  • Recognize and reward people for their effort and contribution.

Openness

We are open to ideas and diversity; we believe that transparency is essential in every aspect of our business.


Measurable Indicators:

  • Share information, experience and knowledge;
  • Communicate with transparency;
  • Comply with all legislation and regulations;
  • Encourage collaboration and teamwork.

Reliability

We do what we say; we deliver on our promises.


Measurable Indicators:

  • Live up to our promises and accept ownership;
  • Take responsibility for action and ensure processes are always in a state of progress;
  • Show respect for time and each deadline that is agreed upon.

Meaning

We bring meaning to our jobs and link them to the organization’s long term goals; we engage in activities that create value for the society and the environment at large.


Measurable Indicators:

  • Actively links own job responsibilities and objectives to the overall vision and long term goals of the organization and helps others do the same.
  • Get involved in activities that add value and prosperity to our society;
  • Show consideration for the effects of actions on others and our society at large;
  • Participate in CSR activities that create a positive impact in our community.
فلسفة نحو الشعب +

Alturki encourages, recognizes and rewards high performers. We treat people fairly and consistently in all matters with a uniform application of the following philosophies:

  • We must offer people careers where they are able to achieve optimum productivity, as well as personal and professional growth;
  • Compensation and benefits are fair, equitable and competitive with the relevant market;
  • We encourage and support our people to develop individually and grow within the overall team;
  • Reward, recognition, and career development is primarily based on performance in accordance with our Culture. Alturki also rewards loyalty and years of service, however, these do not determine career progression;
  • We insist on open and sincere dialog;
  • We maintain a healthy and safe workplace; and
  • We do not discriminate on any basis.

Authority & Responsibility Matrix


About the Authority Matrix - ORA


The acronym ORA stands for:


Letter

Stands for

Definition

O

Originate

Implies discretionary authority in specific business events provided that the decision is in line with adopted policies, rules or regulations of the authority.

R

Review

Refers to the authority that establishes the extent of required review/verification based on risk considerations to certify that the transaction is accurate and genuine. This also includes the authority that applies to exercise the power of recommendation in specific business event.

A

Approve

Applies to the authority that initiates a process/activity and prepares the relevant documents.
