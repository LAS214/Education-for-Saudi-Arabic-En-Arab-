BUSINESS TRAVEL
Definition of a Business Trip +

An approved business related trip from one (1) day up to five (5) days outside the city of residence and the regular place of work is considered a business trip. Trips that exceed 5 days should be approved by CHRO.

Approval of a Business Trip +

All business trips within or outside the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia must be approved in advance from the direct manager as per Delegation of Authority (DoA). The travel application form should contain a detailed time schedule outlining the destinations. If the business trip is coupled with public holidays or vacations, it must be clearly mentioned in the application form. Please ensure that only the authorized business travel destinations are mentioned on this form.

Air Travel +

Appointed travel agent will handle all flight reservations and ticket purchases only. Otherwise, depending on the location, this will be arranged by the department/branch, or the employee him/herself. The Company reserves the right to select the most suitable carrier for business trips of employees paid by the company. In cases where the entitled class is not available, the next lower class will be applied for domestic and international travels.

Lodging +

4.1. Hotel Spending Guidelines

Travelers are required to use Alturki Holding negotiated or preferred rates whenever possible. Travelers will be reimbursed for actual and reasonable hotel room costs (Refer to Table).

4.2. Hotel Accommodations

We expect our employees to stay at suitable, economic hotels near to their intended place of visit at the destination. Alturki Holding will make necessary accommodation arrangements and will pay, if possible, for the hotel room(s) in advance.

5.2. Reimbursement for Personal Car Usage

Travelers will be reimbursed for business usage of personal cars at current Business Mileage and only when driving to destinations outside of their region:

  • SR 0.70 will be paid for every KM above 150K trip;
  • To be reimbursed for use of their personal car for business, travelers must provide on the expense report:
    • Purpose of the trip;
    • Date and location;
    • Receipts for tolls and parking;
  • The employees, being self-responsible, are expected to maintain a record of their traveling distances. At the time of claiming reimbursement, they need to submit a simplified statement for this purpose;
  • This reimbursement amount will cover all maintenance expenses related to the car of the employee e.g. oil, breakdowns, accidents, etc.
Airport Parking +
  • Long-term Parking fees are reimbursed if they don’t exceed 5 calendar days.
  • No “short-term parking area” is allowed at airports.
Combining Personal with Business Travel +

7.1. Personal/vacation travel may be combined with Business Travel

Provided there is no additional cost to the Company, and with approval from the traveler’s manager.

7.2. Spouse/Family Travel Combined With Business Travel

Travel and entertainment expenses incurred by a spouse or other individual accompanying an employee on business are NOT reimbursable.

Meals & Entertainment +

8.1. Personal Meal Expenses

Personal meals are defined as meal expenses incurred by the individual traveler when on an out-of-town business trip, thus are not covered by the company (e.g. hotel booking include breakfast). Personal meal expenses will be reimbursed according to actual and reasonable cost (Refer to Table below).

8.2. Business Meal Expenses

Business Meals are defined as meals taken with customers or suppliers during which a specific business discussion or meeting takes place. Employees will be reimbursed for business meal expenses according to actual and reasonable costs (Refer to Table below).

Other Allowable Travel-Related Expenses +

The following incidental expenses, including the following:

  • Tips/ Gratuities 10% at Restaurants;
  • Parking & Tolls;
  • Express Laundry/Valet Service for trips in excess of 1-day business days, depending on extenuating circumstances (e.g., weather conditions, unexpected business meeting obligations, etc.);
  • Currency Conversion;
  • Remote Internet/Wi-Fi access cost;
  • An employee on travel status will be covered for necessary local and long distance business telephone calls. Use of long distance telephone/mobile calls must be reasonable and supported by receipts/statements. Travelers are advised to use Internet call services that are free (e.g. Skype).
General Entitlements +

10.1. Business Networking Meals

Employees may claim an expense of business lunch/dinner for networking and business development initiatives, provided the purpose is reasonable and the expense will be in acceptable volume and amount. All original invoices must be submitted during the claim with full details of invitation, i.e. purpose, invitees, etc.).

10.2. Personal Entertainment Expenses

Personal entertainment expenses including minibar, in-hotel movies, health club facilities, in-theatre movies, consumption and purchase of alcohol, any form of tips, any kind of social activities and related incidental costs must be borne by the employee.
